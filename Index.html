Muhammad Abubakar

I am a Data Engineer at GWDG (Gesellschaft für Wissenschaftliche Datenverarbeitung mbH) in Göttingen, Germany, where I design and maintain scalable ETL pipelines for multi-modal primate research datasets spanning Thailand, Senegal, and Ethiopia.

I am broadly interested in the intersection of data engineering, machine learning, and scientific computing. My work focuses on building robust, cloud-native data infrastructure — Apache Airflow orchestration, AWS S3 and GPU cluster management — that enables researchers to extract meaningful insights from terabyte-scale heterogeneous datasets.

Previously, I was a Data Engineer at Panther Solutions GmbH in Frankfurt, building ML data pipelines for pricing prediction, and a Machine Learning Engineer at SlimLogix in Lahore, developing computer vision and OCR systems for invoice processing. I am pursuing an M.Sc. in Applied Mathematics for Data Science and Networks at Hochschule Mittweida.
